TATA NEXON

❮
❯

Detailed description of TATA NEXON goes here.

  • Price: The Tata Nexon EV is priced between Rs 14.74 lakh and Rs 19.94 lakh (introductory ex-showroom Delhi).
  • Variants: The electric version of the Nexon facelift can be had in 3 broad variants: Creative, Fearless, and Empowered.
  • Colours: Tata offers the Nexon EV in 7 colour options: Flame Red, Pristine White, Intensi Teal, Empowered Oxide, Fearless Purple, Creative Ocean, and Daytona Grey.
  • Seating Capacity: It is a 5-seater electric subcompact SUV.
  • Battery, Electric Motor & Range: The Nexon EV gets 2 battery pack options: a 30 kWh battery pack paired with an electric motor which makes 129 PS/215 Nm, and gives a claimed range up to 325 km, and the other employs a larger 40.5kWh pack mated to an electric motor that churns out 144 PS/215 Nm, and delivers a claimed range of up to 465 km.
  • Charging: The Nexon EV supports multiple charging options, which are detailed below: 7.2 kW AC Home Charger (10-100 %): 4.3 hours (Medium Range), 6 hours (Long Range) AC Home Wallbox (10-100 %): 10.5 hours (Medium Range), 15 hours (Long Range) DC Fast Charger (10-100 %): 56 minutes for both 15 A Portable Charger (10-100 %): 10.5 hours (Medium Range), 15 hours (Long Range)
  • Features: Features on board the Nexon EV include a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, a 10.25-inch fully digital driver’s display, a 9-speaker JBL sound system, automatic AC, cruise control, ventilated front seats, wireless phone charging, and a single-pane sunroof. It also comes with vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) and vehicle-to-load (V2L) functionalities.
  • Safety: On the safety front, it gets 6 airbags as standard, ABS with EBD, electronic stability control (ESC), a 360-degree camera, a blind view monitoring system, and front and rear parking sensors.
