KIA EV 6

❮
❯

Detailed description of KIA EV 6 goes here.

  • Price: Kia has priced the EV6 from Rs 60.95 lakh to Rs 65.95 lakh (ex-showroom pan India).
  • Variants: The electric SUV comes in a single top-of-the-line GT trim. There are 2 variants to choose from: GT Line RWD and GT Line AWD.
  • Seating Capacity: It is offered in a 5-seater configuration.
  • Battery Pack, Electric Motor and Range: The India-spec Kia EV6 gets a 77.4 kWh battery pack and the following powertrain choices: a single motor rear-wheel drive (229 PS/350 Nm), and a dual motor all-wheel drive (325 PS/605 Nm) setup. The EV6 has an ARAI-claimed range of up to 708 km.
  • Charging: Its battery can be rejuvenated from 10 to 80 percent in 18 minutes using a 350 kW DC fast charger. It takes 73 minutes to juice it up from 10 to 80 percent using a 50 kW DC fast charger while a home charger does the same task in 36 hours.
  • >Features: The EV6 boasts amenities like curved 12.3-inch displays for the instrument cluster and touchscreen infotainment system, a 14-speaker Meridian sound system, wireless phone charging, dual-zone climate control, ventilated and powered front seats, and a single-pane sunroof.
  • Safety: It gets 8 airbags, electronic stability control (ESC) and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) like automatic emergency braking, adaptive cruise control, lane-keep assist and blind spot monitoring.
