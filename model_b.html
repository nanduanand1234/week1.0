Detailed description of MG ZS EV goes here.

  • Price: The MG ZS EV is priced between Rs 22.88 lakh and Rs 26 lakh (ex-showroom pan India).
  • Variants: It is sold in three broad variants: Excite, Exclusive, and Exclusive Pro.
  • Seating Capacity: MG offers it in a 5-seater configuration.
  • Colours: The electric SUV is available in four colour options: Glaze Red, Aurora Silver, Starry Black and Candy White.
  • Battery Pack, Electric Motor and Range: MG has equipped it with a 50.3kWh battery pack and an electric motor making 177 PS and 280 Nm. The MG EV has a claimed range of 461 km.
  • Charging: The ZS EV can be fully charged in around 8.5 to nine hours using a 7.4kW AC charger, while a 50kW DC fast charger can refill the battery from 0-80 per cent in just 60 minutes.
  • Features: MG offers the ZS EV with a 10-inch touchscreen infotainment system, a 7-inch digital driver’s display, a panoramic sunroof, and a powered driver seat. The electric SUV is also provided with connected car tech and a wireless phone charger.
  • Safety: Its safety net includes six airbags, ABS with EBD, electronic stability control, a 360-degree camera, and hill descent control. MG even offers it with a suite of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) including lane keep assist and departure warning, adaptive cruise control, traffic jam assist, and autonomous emergency braking.
